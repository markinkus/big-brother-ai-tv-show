# Product Rules

- Neural House is a state-driven social simulation with an entertainment layer.
- Deterministic world rules own the truth; language generation decorates, interprets, and compresses state.
- Persona cards are fictional role blueprints, not real-person identity claims.
- Cultural, historical, and religious framing must remain respectful, non-hateful, and non-degrading.
- VIP access is premium-only in architecture, but MVP billing can be stubbed.
- Newsroom output is a real narrative system, not decorative filler.
